Festival Timeline

4 February 2016

Program Go Green

Program Go Green was held by class upper 6 Al Farabi at 4 February 2016.This program is one of the activities that was designed for the MUET subjects.The objective of the program is to raise awareness of environmental conservation among students.

31 March 2016

Go Green Campaign series 2/2016

Once again the Pre-university MUET Unit successfully held the second series of Go Green Campaign. This time the program involves classes 6 Atas Al Biruni, 6 Atas Al Alusi and 6 Atas Al Jauzi. A number of activities were carried out including the exhibition of posters and recycled products. Congratulations!

23 April 2025

Recycling Practice Talk

On 26 March 2018, a talk was held organized by the school in collaboration with SWM Environment Sdn Bhd for all students in the afternoon session. The lecture was delivered by Encik Shahrul Amir bin Abd Rahim,
Senior Executive Corporate Communication from SWM Environment Sdn Bhd. The WMAM Eco Challenge 2018 program was also held from school to international level. All students are welcome to participate in the video competition that promises handsome rewards to the winners.

18 April 2018

Program Go Green Bersama UniKL

The UniKL Go Green program was implemented with the residents of Asrama Pusat Kota Tinggi (APKT) on 17 April 2018. The event started at 8.00 am and ended at 5.00 pm. A total of 12 facilitators from UniKL were involved in the program led by Brother Muhamad Amirul Asyraf bin Hamdan who is a former student of SMK Tun Habab. The closing ceremony was completed by Puan Juriah binti Jaafar, Deputy Superintendent of the Hostel with the presence of a lecturer from UniKL Mr. Muhammad Akmal Hakim bin Hazman.

23 April 2025

Recycling Program

A recycling program was held to fill activity slots after the end-of-year examination on 14 November 2018. All students involved in the program except students in form 5 and form 6 of STPM 2018 carried out activities to collect discarded materials and separate them according to the categories of plastic, paper and aluminum . Congratulations to all students.

About

Go Green Campaign is a website that aim to population at SMK Tun Habab in order to gain knowledge as the first step to the green lifestyle.
